Adaptability

Nagios Log Server has a fully accessible API allowing for complete integration to fit the needs of your external applications. Nagios Log Server easily integrates with third-party solutions or your current infrastructure.

Customized Dashboards

A powerful GUI provides for customization of layout, design, and preferences on a per-user basis, giving your customers and team members the flexibility they want. Users can create custom dashboards in the web-interface to see quick views of data most important to them. Users can also easily share dashboards with a custom URL to enhance team collaboration.
